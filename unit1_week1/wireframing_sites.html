Rough Wire Frames of Sites That I Like:

Apple.com -- solves the problem of keeping people informed about the latest Apple products, also allows them to buy said products online, and connect current users to technical support related to their Apple products.

Google.com -- serves as both a search engine - connecting users via an algorithm to relevant results based on terms that users search, as well as a gateway to user's Google account (Google Plus, Gmail, Google Calendar etc...)

Reddit.com -- is an online community where users can submit links to content (videos, images, links, forums, blogs, etc.) that they think is meaningful/interesting and allows others to comment on said content. It is a news site and forum rolled into one.
