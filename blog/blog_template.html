Thoughts on Feedback

One of the things that DBC's ciriculum focuses on is feedback, as they consider feedback to be integral to advancing one's own learning. In fact, DBC stresses that a tight (as in small) feedback loop is integral to rapid learning.

Nevertheless, a very important part of feedback is how one give's and receives feedback to and from others.
